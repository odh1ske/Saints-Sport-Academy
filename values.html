Our Core Values

  1. Discipline – Commitment to rules, routine, and self-control.
  2. Respect – For teammates, coaches, referees, parents, and opponents.
  3. Integrity – Honesty, fairness, and strong character.
  4. Teamwork – Winning together and supporting each other.
  5. Excellence – Giving our best every day.
  6. Sportsmanship – Humility in victory and courage in defeat.
  7. Resilience – Learning from challenges and never giving up.
  8. Passion – Loving the game and playing with joy.
  9. Inclusivity – Equal opportunities for all.
  10. Leadership & Community – Using sport to create positive impact.
